About Us

Farm2Fork is a cross-platform mobile application that enlightens you with information about where your food comes from. By simply either scanning or entering the QR Code found on the packaging of your food, this application can inform you about the farm from whence it came, the route the food traveled from the farm to the market and also about the source of the food itself - whether that be the animal or crop it came from.

Farm2Fork was developed by University of Strathclyde CS317 Group 21:
Liam Diamond, Paul Burns, William Tetlow, Shannon Fitzpatrick and Craig Morrison
